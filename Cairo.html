Places To Stay

  • Four Seasons Cairo At Nile Plaza - A Five Star luxurious experience with top quality hospitality. Next to the famous Nile River.

  • Cairo Conrad Hotel - An elegant and sophisticated place to stay offering everything from four star dining to Le Bar which overlooks the Nile

  • Mena House Hotel - For a more authentically Egyptian and luxurious stay. The decor and surrounding are unique and unforgettable. Rich with historic interiors and rooms that overlook the pyramids
