Philadelphia

The city of Brotherly Love held up to it's name. Filled with friendly and vibrant people who turn the city electric when their sports teams come to town. Never experience a dull moment as there are numerous landmarks to visit.

Cairo

A city rich in history, artifacts and epic monuments. There was so much to see, do and eat that a week was too short. Surrounded by desert, the scenery is both amazing and unique. A visit to the well-known Khan-el-Khalili market will leave you mesmerised.

Hurghada

Most adventurous holiday I have experienced thus far. So close to the red sea, this is a destination for divers and water sports enthusiasts. There is also the desert which gives the opportunity to gear up the quadbikes and take rides on camels and horses.
